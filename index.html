📝 Frequently Asked Questions

?What if my tool has an issue?⌄

If it shows errors, that usually means you have not yet added data. Make sure to click File > Make a copy to own it and get full features. Reach out to live chat support if the above does not help. If you are reaching out during off-duty hours, please email ai@text2tool.com and a ticket will be created. Someone will get back to you within 12 hours or less.

✎How should I write my prompt?⌄

Keep it short, clear, and simple. Example: “Create me a profit loss tracker for my Shopify business.”

⚡Do I need to explain formulas and tabs?⌄

No. Text2Tool is designed to create the best structure from simple prompts. You can add details only when they are truly important.

✉How will I receive my tool?⌄

Your tool is exported to Google Sheets and sent to you via email in 5–7 minutes. Make sure to click File > Make a copy before using it so you get ownership.

✓Do I own the Google Sheet after delivery?⌄

Yes. Click File → Make a copy to save the tool inside your own Google Drive and use it under your own Google account.

⚙Can I edit the tool after making a copy?⌄

Yes. First open the delivered Google Sheet, then click File > Make a copy. After the copy is saved in your own Google Drive, you can edit and customize the tool according to your needs.

▦What kinds of tools can I request?⌄

You can request invoice generators, attendance trackers, inventory trackers, lead trackers, commission calculators, payroll calculators, KPI dashboards, profit and loss trackers, and similar Google Sheet tools.

$What are Tool Kits?⌄

Tool Kits are used when you are ready to generate the final working Google Sheets tool after previewing the idea.
