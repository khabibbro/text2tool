What is an AI spreadsheet builder?

An AI spreadsheet builder helps turn a plain language request into a usable spreadsheet. Instead of starting from an empty sheet, you describe the business tool you need and Text2Tool prepares the structure for you.

It is useful when you need a working spreadsheet faster than building all tabs, formulas, dropdowns, reports and dashboards manually.
